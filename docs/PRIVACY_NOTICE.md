# Biometric attendance privacy notice template

**Purpose:** Facial data is used only to verify your identity when you choose to record attendance. It is not used for surveillance, identification of unknown people, performance measurement, ranking, discipline, or employment decisions.

**Data:** Live captures are processed to create or compare a mathematical template. Raw captures are deleted immediately by default. The template is encrypted separately from your employee profile.

**Choice:** Enrollment is voluntary. You may always use employee ID and PIN or request administrator-assisted attendance. A failed face check will not mark you absent.

**Access and retention:** Only specifically authorized administrators may manage enrollment state; they cannot view or download templates. The default template retention period is 365 days and must be adjusted to applicable law and policy.

**Your controls:** You may withdraw consent, request re-enrollment, request access/export, or request deletion. Some attendance and audit records may be retained where legally required. Contact the organization’s privacy officer for questions or complaints.
