# Employee guide

Sign in with your employee ID/email and password. Record check-in, check-out, and breaks with either face verification or PIN. If the camera is unavailable or verification fails, choose PIN or ask an administrator for assistance; a failure does not make you absent. Use **My history** to review records and request a correction. Use **Face & consent** to review consent, re-enroll, request an export, or request deletion. You cannot directly change managed fields or attendance history.
